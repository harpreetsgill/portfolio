Hi,


I am Harpreet. I am a student of New Media Design & Web Development at British Columbia Institute of Technology (BCIT) where I am learning how to bring ideas into reality through different mediums that range from posters, logos, user interfaces to static and dynamic websites.


I have acquired the foundation skills of graphic design and front-end web development and now I continue to build on them more complex ideas that are aesthetically pleasing and functional.


I can't exactly put into words what interests me about design and web development. It is a combination of several things. The loss of track of time when working on some projects, that feeling when I have imagined something a few days or weeks back and now I can see it's the final output, improving something with each iteration, when something doesn't end up looking how I imagined it but it's different and how my perception of the project then changes or trying to adjust the project according to my initial idea, improving the lives of people in ways which they don't realize.


I am enjoying this journey and intend to keep my passion alive by working on projects that interest me.
