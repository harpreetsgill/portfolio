Objective

This was a self-led project to design and develop a website for the Stearman Point Brewery. I had a complete brand identity from another project. I wanted to extend that and give the brewery an online presence. The purpose was to make a desktop version of the website that follows the design language of the brand.

Rough Sketches and Wireframes

On paper, I started making rough wireframes to get an idea of where all the content would go on the homepage.

Refining the rough wireframes

I then started designing the homepage in Adobe Xd. What made sense on paper didn't look good here. After a few variations and a lot of inspiration from Fort Point Beer Company's website design, things started to fall into place.

Hi-Fi Mockups

Based on the homepage, I designed other pages. After designing the homepage, I started coding too. I was going back and forth between designing and coding to make sense of what works on both ends. I skipped the mockup of some pages as I had a clear idea about the way I wanted the page to look and also because I was running out of time.

Live Preview

The final website did end up looking as if it is part of the same brand. I have plans to add more interactivity and some functionality to the website until I am completely satisfied with it.
