Random Posters

Field

Graphic Design

Tools

Adobe Photoshop

Date

Feb 2020

Objective

I gave myself a maximum of 2 hours for designing each poster. In 6 of the posters, I picked random topics from Wikipedia's Random article page. The posters are meant to incite the user to read more on the topic by visiting the Wikipedia page.

In the other 6, each poster represents a feeling or emotion. I tried incorporating symbolism in some of these.
