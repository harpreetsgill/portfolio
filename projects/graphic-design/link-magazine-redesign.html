Field

Graphic Design

Tools

Adobe InDesign

Adobe Photoshop

Date

Nov 2019

PDF

Objective

This was a project in Term 1. The purpose was to redesign the layout of a newsletter or a magazine. I chose BCIT's Link Magazine. I took articles from different issues of the magazine and redesigned the layout and images on those pages.

Brainstorming

The first thing that I did was put on paper all the ideas that went through my mind.

Then I started eliminating less interesting ideas and selected the one I felt I could work on.

The Final Design

Then I went straight into Photoshop and InDesign and started experimenting with text, colours and images.

All the articles are written by the writers mentioned in the magazine. I have not edited the articles.
