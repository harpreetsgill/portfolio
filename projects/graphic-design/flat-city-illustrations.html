Flat City Illustrations

Field

Graphic Design

Tools

Adobe Illustrator

Date

June 2020

Result

Objective

For the Illustration class, we were required to make a flat city illustration. One of the designs here was done for the course and the other is self-led.

Reference Images

For the first design, I picked an Indian city called Varanasi. For the second design, I chose an image of a favela in Brazil.

Source: Image 1, Image 2

The Process

Everything is made using basic shapes and then transformed using the Direct Selection Tool. Each building was put into a layer of its own.
