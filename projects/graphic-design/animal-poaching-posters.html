Result

Brainstorming ideas

This process of this project is the continuation of the Exotic Animal's Exhibition project. I did not end up submitting that project and decided to explore more of these ideas.

After some experimentation

I came up with this after playing around with the brush tool a little.
I didn't know what it meant. It seemed like a businessman introspecting. I really liked the design and wanted to make an event around it.

Trying to tie them together

This was supposed to be about a swimmer. The buildings' silhouettes didn't make much sense.

I tried putting it all together

But the last two didn't fit with the first one. This was about a music composer. Perhaps the music composer was going to talk about his process or something else. I wasn't convinced with this series.
But there were things that I really liked about it. So I took them and played with it a little more.

final_final_ akheerversion4

This is what I finally submitted for the project.
This was about a journalist who was killed while researching an animal poaching story in Kenya.
Her unfinished stories were going to be published by a publishing agency called Unit Books.
