Stearman Point Brewery Branding

Field

Graphic Design

Tools

Adobe Illustrator

Adobe InDesign

Adobe Photoshop

Date

June 2020

Result

Objective

This was a project for the Illustrator for New Media course in Term 2. We were required to design a brand identity for a fictional beverage company and design the packaging label for the product.

Choosing a name

I randomly browsed around the map of B.C. to look for a name. After a while, I came across a place called Stearman Beach. I decided to use that name by adding Point instead of Beach at the end.

Moodboard

To define the voice of the brand and the direction I wanted to head towards, I started collecting images that had a similar style.

For all the images, visit the Pinterest Board.

Rough Exploration

I decided on using a lighthouse and waves in the design but I didn't have any idea how this would fit in or where it would go.

Logo Ideas

Based on the lighthouse and the waves, I started experimenting with the S and the circular logo that would act as the secondary logo for the brand.

Brand Identity

The waves in the S to represent the ocean and the red upper half of the S to represent the lighthouse. The dot for Point.
For the logotype Brut Gothic was a perfect fit. The way it blends boldness, history and humbleness suited the brand's voice.

Can Design

For the can design, I traced a can image and also used a real can to understand the shape of the can.
Using the Revolve tool in Illustrator, I made the can and the opening of the can.

Label Design Exploration

I wanted to incorporate the map grid into the design but it wasn't working. I couldn't figure out what wasn't working for a few days. Then I decided to remove the grid. It was a difficult decision since I had thought of using it since the beginning. Instead, I used the +(pluses) where the lines of the grid intersect. Now, it looked a lot less congested.

Final Label Design

After applying the brand colours, this is what I ended up with.

Poster

The brand identity, can and the label design, all came together in this poster that promotes the bear and the brand.

Stationery Design

In Term 3, for a stationery design assignment, I chose this brand and designed the business card, envelope and letterhead using Adobe InDesign.

The mock-up design was downloaded from here. I edited it in Photoshop and inserted my designs as smart objects.

More Label Designs

In Term 3, I expanded the project a little and designed 7 more labels. The design is the same. They just have different colours.
