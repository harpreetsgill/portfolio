Exotic Animals' Exhibition Posters

Field

Graphic Design

Tools

Adobe Photoshop

Adobe InDesign

Date

Dec 2019

Result

Brainstorming ideas

These are all the ideas that popped into my head when the project was assigned. They all seemed great and I wanted to pursue every one of them.

So, I went ahead and tried a few of them

This was like a propaganda poster in an alternate reality where Nazis took control over the world. I tried using very odd colours and random geometric shapes to give a sense that this is something we are not familiar with.

An attempt to focus on a single topic

I was really excited and was getting so many ideas about what I could make. It felt overwhelming too. This was like an attempt to shut up my mind and pick a topic and choose.

Inspiration

I decided to do something with grids. I had recently learned about the Liquify tool in Photoshop and I wanted to do something with it. I had also seen some posters where grids were distorted and I thought that was really cool.

The Final Design

These are the final 3 posters that I ended up with. It's about an exhibition that showcases exotic animals from around the world. Some of the animals have been modified.
The audience gets to watch the animals very closely. The liquifying/deforming of the grid is used to convey the immersion that the exhibition will provide.
