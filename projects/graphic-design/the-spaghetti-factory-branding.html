The Spaghetti Factory's Brand Identity Design

Field

Graphic Design

Tools

Adobe Illustrator

Date

Mar 2020

Result

Objective

The purpose of this project was to design a brand identity for a fictional business. I created The Spaghetti Factory, a chain restaurant business largely inspired by The Old Spaghetti Factory.
Another requirement was to design the UI for the homepage of the website.

Moodboard

As soon as I saw the name "The Spaghetti Factory", I knew that something could be done with the words of the brand name.

These are the reference images that I used to base the new design on.

Sketches

While sketching, I tried to combine three things into one:

1. Factory
2. Fork
3. Spaghetti

Colours

Primary Colours:
Dark Brown - to represent the chimney of the restaurant and some of the very tiny ingredients in spaghetti.
Light Orange - to represent the colour of the spaghetti.

Secondary Colours:
Red - for the tomatoes and the spices that go in the food
Blue - to balance all the energy with something cool
Light Yellow - to give a mixed feeling of old and new

Typography

For the headings, Open Sans is used.

For the sub-headings, Cooper Hewitt.

For all the body text, PT Sans is used.

Homepage UI Design

Utilizing all the colours in the colour palette and the different fonts, the homepage was designed.

A lot of white space and the basic shapes were used to give a clean and modern look.
